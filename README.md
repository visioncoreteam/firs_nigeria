# 🇳🇬 FIRS GO! — Nigeria E-Invoicing Web Service

> **Streamline Your E-Invoicing Process with Ease**

FIRS GO! is a user-friendly web service designed to seamlessly connect the **Manager Cloud Accounting** app with the FIRS E-Invoicing system. With FIRS GO!, you can generate and send electronic invoices more easily than ever before.

---

## 📌 What is FIRS?

FIRS is Nigeria's pioneering national electronic invoicing system, introduced through a visionary partnership between the **Nigeria Revenue Service (NRS)** and the **Merchant Buyer Solution (MBS)**. Designed to revolutionize the country's fiscal landscape, FIRS seeks to:

- ✅ Elevate transparency in sales and purchase transactions.
- ✅ Simplify tax data collection for the NRS with seamless efficiency.
- ✅ Modernize Nigeria's tax infrastructure by embracing cutting-edge digital invoicing solutions.

## 🚀 How to Start?

To get started, set up **two extensions** inside your **Manager** accounting application.

Navigate to **Settings › Extensions** and create the following two extensions exactly as shown:

---

### 🧾 1. Sales Invoice Extension

| Field       | Value                          |
|-------------|-------------------------------|
| **Name**    | `INVOICE_FIRS`                |
| **Source**  | URL                           |
| **Endpoint**| `https://firs.visioncorego.com` |
| **Placement**| `/sales-invoice-view`        |

---

### 📋 2. Credit Note Extension

| Field       | Value                          |
|-------------|-------------------------------|
| **Name**    | `CREDITNOTE_FIRS`             |
| **Source**  | URL                           |
| **Endpoint**| `https://firs.visioncorego.com` |
| **Placement**| `/credit-note-view`          |

---

## ✅ After Setting Up the Extensions

Once both extensions have been successfully created, navigate to any **Sales Invoice** or **Credit Note** within Manager.

You will notice a new extension button displayed at the bottom of the document view. Clicking this button will launch the **FIRS GO!** e-invoicing process directly within that document, allowing you to generate and submit your e-invoice with ease.
